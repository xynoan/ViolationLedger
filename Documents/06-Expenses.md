### 1. Camera
- **Amount:** ₱2,000.00
- **Purpose:** Camera equipment for illegal parking detection
- **Type:** One-time purchase

### 2. AI Service
- **Amount:** ₱3,500.00
- **Purpose:** AI detection service using Google Gemini API
- **Type:** Service subscription/usage

### 3. SMS Semaphore Credits
- **Amount:** ₱2,500.00
- **Credits:** 5,000 SMS credits

- **Type:** One-time registration fee

### 5. Hardware Camera Post
- **Amount:** ₱1,000.00
- **Purpose:** Camera mounting post and installation hardware
- **Type:** One-time purchase
